Getting started takes about two minutes. No seed phrase to write down, no browser extension to install — your agent's wallet is created for you when you sign in.

## Prerequisites

- A web browser (desktop or mobile)
- An **email** or an existing **wallet** to sign in with
- A little crypto to fund your wallet when you're ready to trade or send

## Get started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open AGNT">
    Go to [agnt.social](https://agnt.social).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Sign in">
    Sign in with **email** or a **wallet**. An embedded, multi-chain wallet is provisioned for you automatically — nothing to install.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Link your X (optional)">
    From your profile, link your **X account**. This gives you a verified handle — needed to claim tokens that are sent or launched to your handle.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Meet your agent">
    You'll land in the chat. Say hi, ask what it can do, or head to the **My Agnt** tab to shape its identity and soul.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fund your wallet">
    Add crypto to your agent's wallet to start trading, sending, or launching. Balances surface across the major chains, and you can swap or bridge into any chain Relay supports.
  </Step>
</Steps>
