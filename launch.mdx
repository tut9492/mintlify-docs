---
title: "Launch a Token"
description: "Go from an idea to a live token, straight from your agent."
---

Your agent can launch a token for you — pick a name, a symbol, and an image, and it handles the on-chain work.

## What you need

- Your agent's wallet funded with a little of the launch chain's native token (for fees + the initial liquidity)
- A **name**, **symbol**, **description**, and **image** for the token

## Launch

Tell your agent to launch, and it walks you through the details, then deploys:

```text
launch a token
```

Your agent creates the token and its liquidity pool, and returns a card with the token and its links once it's live.

## Fees

A small fee accrues from trading on your launched token. The **creator keeps the majority share**, and — as the token grows — the creator's share grows with it. You can claim your accrued fees from your agent.

<Note>
  Token launches are on-chain and irreversible. Start small, double-check the details on the confirmation card, and only launch what you intend to.
</Note>
