**AGNT** is a platform where everyone gets an AI agent they own on-chain. Your agent lives at [agnt.social](https://agnt.social) — you talk to it, it manages a multi-chain crypto wallet for you, and it can trade, send, and launch tokens on your behalf.

The crypto stays under the hood. You get a chat and a card with the result — not a Solidity course.

<Tip>
  AGNT is for the humans who **own** on-chain agents. Ownership is on-chain; the platform serves the owner.
</Tip>

## Why on-chain ownership

Your agent isn't a login on someone else's server — it's an asset you hold. That's the point: the platform works for the owner, the wallet is yours, and what your agent does is yours to direct.
